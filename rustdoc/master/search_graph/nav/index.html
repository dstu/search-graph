search_graph

Module search_graph::nav [−] [src]

Support for navigation of a graph without allowing modifications to graph topology.

The data structures in this module require a read-only borrow of an underlying graph. It is safe to navigate through a graph with such multiple structures pointing into it. Graph data that may be updated safely through read-only references (such as atomic types and std::cell::RefCell) may be modified through these structures.
